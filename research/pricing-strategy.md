*Last updated: April 5, 2026*

## Our Pricing Tiers

### Standard Designs (text-based, single-color) — $24.99
- Cost: ~$9-11 (Printify Bella Canvas 3001)
- Margin: ~$14-16 (56-64%)
- Best for: Funny phrases, sarcasm, profession-based

### Premium Designs (illustrated, multi-color) — $29.99
- Cost: ~$11-13 (Printify premium blank)
- Margin: ~$17-19 (57-63%)
- Best for: Retro illustrations, vintage art, complex graphics

### Featured/Limited Designs — $34.99
- Cost: ~$11-13
- Margin: ~$22-24 (63-69%)
- Best for: Trending viral designs, seasonal limited runs

## Free Shipping Strategy
- Threshold: $35 (encourages 2-shirt purchases at $24.99 + $24.99 = $49.98)
- Single shirt shipping: $5.99
- Psychology: "Add one more shirt for free shipping" drives AOV

## Key Insights
- Text-only shirts rarely exceed $35 price ceiling
- Illustrated designs can command $50+ but lower conversion
- Black/white/navy shirts cost less to produce AND sell more
- Personalized designs (names, dates) command 15-25% premium
- Seasonal designs can be priced 10-15% higher during peak
