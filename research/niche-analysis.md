## Design Style Rankings (by conversion)
1. **Text + Image Mashup** — Highest engagement (image creates context, text delivers punchline)
2. **Bold Typography** — Consistent profitability, evergreen
3. **Niche-Specific References** — Loyal audience, less competition
4. **Retro/Vintage Illustration** — Premium feel, nostalgia
5. **Minimalist/Clean** — Modern aesthetic, works on all blanks
6. **Complex Graphics Alone** — Lower conversion but higher price ceiling ($50+)

## Color Insights
- Black, White, Navy = 60% of all shirt sales
- Heather Gray strong for unisex
- Pastel demand growing (Gen Z)
- Dark shirts + white/bright text = highest visibility
