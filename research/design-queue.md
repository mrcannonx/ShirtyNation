## DESIGN GENERATION NOTES

### GPT-5.4 Prompt Template:
```
Create a t-shirt design on a transparent background. The design should be [STYLE].
Text: "[PHRASE]"
Style: [bold typography / retro illustration / minimalist / vintage / pop art]
Colors: [specify based on concept — most shirts are white/light design on dark blank OR dark design for light blank]
Mood: [funny / motivational / nostalgic / cute / edgy]
Format: PNG, 4500x5400px (standard Printify print area), 300 DPI
```

### Design Rules:
- ALWAYS transparent background
- Design centered for front-of-shirt placement
- Text must be readable at arm's length
- Max 2-3 colors for text designs (prints cleaner)
- Illustrations can use more colors but keep it clean
- Test on both black AND white shirt mockups before publishing

### Pricing by Type:
- Text-only designs: $24.99
- Text + small illustration: $24.99
- Full illustration: $29.99
- Detailed/premium art: $34.99
