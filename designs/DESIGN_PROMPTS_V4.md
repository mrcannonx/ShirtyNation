# AudacityTees — 50 Illustration-Heavy Designs (v4)

## Design Philosophy
Every design is now **graphic-first, text-minimal**. The illustration does the talking. Text is max 1-4 words — short enough that Ideogram nails it, or you add it in Canva in 30 seconds. These designs pop in Etsy thumbnails because the VISUAL hooks attention, not the text.

## Ideogram Settings
- **Style:** Design
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5
- **Export:** Transparent PNG (Plus/Pro plan)
- Add `isolated on transparent background, no mockup, no shirt, print-ready` to every prompt

## Canva Post-Processing
For any design that needs text added after:
1. Export Ideogram graphic as transparent PNG
2. Open in Canva, place graphic, add text below/above
3. Export final PNG with transparent background

## Why These Work Better
1. **Thumbnail power** — illustrations catch eyes in Etsy search, text doesn't
2. **Ideogram's strength** — generates illustrations cleanly, struggles with long text
3. **Canva for text** — add 1-4 words in Canva with perfect fonts in 30 seconds
4. **Versatile** — same illustration works on multiple shirt colors
5. **Shareable** — graphic designs get shared on social media more than text shirts
6. **Two-step is fast** — Ideogram graphic + Canva text = done in under 2 minutes per design
