# AudacityTees — 50 Ideogram Prompts (v3)

## Settings for EVERY prompt
- **Style:** Design
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5
- **Plan:** Plus or Pro (for transparent PNG export)
- If transparency fails, use remove.bg or Ideogram Canvas eraser after

## Color Rule
- Dark design (white/cream text) → for dark shirts (black, navy, charcoal)
- Dark design version: use white (#FFFFFF) or cream (#F0E6D6) elements
- Light design (black/dark text) → for light shirts (white, cream, heather)
- Make BOTH versions of text-only designs to double your listings
